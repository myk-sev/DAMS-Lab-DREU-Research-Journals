## Goals
The comletion goals for this week are:
1. Collect a test of images from a local hallway/set of classrooms for testing of the localization algorithm.
2. Implement the portion of the pipeline dedicated to generating semantic maps.
3. Test localization on the set of data mentioned above.

The learning goals for the week are:
1. Explore past approaches for utilizing SLAM for assisting individuals with visual impairments. ([paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.20549))
2. Learn how key frame selection can be used to optimize the resource use of the localizing step. ([paper](https://www.mdpi.com/2218-6581/12/3/88))
3. Explore approaches in using text to close loops in maps. ([paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2410.15869))

## Approach and Implementation
With the structure of the pipeline planned and research the next step is implementation. This will be broken into pieces to ensure that a working product is produced by the end of the program. The first and most critical of these pieces will be algorithm that generated the map. Before adding any optimizations or tackling a specific research gap it is necessary to ensure that the core components work and can be fit on a mobile device.

With the final approach chosen a plan will also be created on how to implement this over the coming weeks.
